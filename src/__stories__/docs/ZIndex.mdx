## Why so few layers

Most overlays in Meda use portals or `@base-ui/react` primitives that manage their own stacking context. The tokens here describe shell-owned layering only.

For dropdowns, dialogs, tooltips, and toasts, prefer the primitive's stacking behavior over raw z-index values.
